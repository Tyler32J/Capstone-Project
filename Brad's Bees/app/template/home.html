My Story

Hi, I'm Brad, and I've been working with bees for over 15 years. What started as a childhood fascination with these incredible creatures turned into a lifelong passion and career.

I founded Brad's Bees to provide safe, humane bee removal services while also sharing the amazing benefits of pure, raw honey with the community. Every hive I work with teaches me something new about these remarkable pollinators.

When I'm not removing bees from unwanted locations or harvesting honey, I'm educating others about the vital role bees play in our ecosystem. It's more than just a job - it's my mission to protect both people and pollinators.

Whether you need emergency bee removal or want to enjoy the finest local honey, I'm here to help with expertise, care, and a commitment to doing things right.
