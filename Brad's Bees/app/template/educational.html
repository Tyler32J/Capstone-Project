Education & Outreach

Inspiring the next generation to appreciate and protect our vital pollinators through hands-on learning experiences

Our Mission

At Brad's Bees, we believe education is the key to conservation. Through interactive presentations, live demonstrations, and community workshops, we teach students and adults about the critical role bees play in our ecosystem and how we can all help protect these essential pollinators.

Educational Programs

School Visits

Interactive classroom presentations for grades K-12, featuring live observation hives, bee specimens, and age-appropriate activities.

  • 45-60 minute presentations
  • Hands-on learning activities
  • Q&A sessions with Brad

Community Workshops

Monthly workshops open to the public covering beekeeping basics, pollinator gardens, and sustainable practices.

  • Beginner beekeeping courses
  • Pollinator garden design
  • Honey harvesting demos

Apiary Tours

Guided tours of our working apiary where visitors can observe beekeeping operations and learn about hive management.

  • Small group tours (max 15)
  • Safety equipment provided
  • See bees up close safely

Library Programs

Summer reading programs and special events at local libraries featuring storytelling, crafts, and bee education.

  • All ages welcome
  • Themed activities
  • Free attendance

Hives for Heroes

Supporting veterans through beekeeping therapy and mentorship programs that promote wellness and community connection.

  • Veteran beekeeping training
  • Therapeutic benefits
  • Community building

Custom Events

Tailored presentations for birthday parties, corporate events, farmers markets, and community festivals.

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Custom content
  • All group sizes
