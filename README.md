# Brad’s Bees

This repository contains the official website for **Brad’s Bees**, a professional beekeeping service specializing in safe bee removal, pollination services, and hive setup & management.  
The project provides Brad with a clean, modern, and trustworthy online presence that reflects the quality of his work.

---

## 🐝 About Brad’s Bees
Brad is an experienced beekeeper serving homeowners, farmers, and local businesses.  
His mission is to protect bees, support agriculture, and provide safe, humane solutions for bee-related needs.

---

## 🌟 Services Included

### 🐝 Bee Removal
- Safe and humane removal  
- Protects both the bees and the property  
- Suitable for homes, sheds, barns, and other buildings  

### 🌼 Pollination Services
- Supports crop growth and yield  
- Ideal for farms, orchards, and gardens  
- Helps maintain healthy local ecosystems  

### 🏡 Hive Setup & Management
- Professional hive installation  
- Ongoing hive maintenance  
- Perfect for new beekeepers or property owners wanting sustainable pollination
